ABOUT ME

I, a Dundee native, am not only a passionate photographer but also a loving mother and an avid traveler. With a background in photography and a lifelong connection to her hometown, my journey has been an inspiring one. I specialize in capturing the beauty of nature through her lens, but her versatility extends to various genres to sustain her livelihood.

Welcome to my world of photography, where I blend my roles as a mother and a globetrotter with my artistic vision. Through my lens, I aim to share the wonders of the natural world and the diverse experiences that life offers. Join me on this visual voyage and explore the beauty through my eyes.
